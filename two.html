About Us

We do it for Passion!

We believe in music. Music is known to be the language of the universe and we have felt it. Everything has music, even the celestial bodies. It is a way of communicating what we cannot express in words. Where words fail, music speaks. It transformed our life, filled it with magic and gave our souls an irreplaceable experience.

Here at Raga Academy, our sole vision is to help others experience this beautiful feeling. We offer a variety of courses that you can choose from. Music is all about arranging sounds in time to produce a composition through the elements of melody, harmony, rhythm, and timbre. We help you in your magical and musical journey of exploring yourself through music.

Music is Alchemy!
Persuade it as a hobby, or make careers in it, or just do it to relax your mind, find and transform yourself.
It is therapy for your soul you did not know you needed.
